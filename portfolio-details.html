Loan Optimizing and Risk Management

Project information

  • Tools: SQL for data manipulation and Power-BI for visualization
  • Key Concepts: Data Aggregation & Summarization, KPIs, Customer Segmentation, Date & Time Analysis
  • Project URL: www.github.com
  • Interactive Dashboard: www.powerbi.com

The bank's loan department seeks to minimize the risk associated with loan repayment by leveraging historical and current loan application data. Efficient loan processing and risk management are critical in the competitive financial sector. By analyzing borrower demographics, financial details, loan terms, loan status, purpose, and repayment behavior, the bank aims to enhance decision-making processes for future loans.
This project highlights my ability to apply data-driven strategies to optimize financial operations and mitigate risks
