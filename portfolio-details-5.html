Travel & Tour Churn Analysis

Project information

  • Tool: Power BI
  • Skills demonstrated: ETL, Data Modelling, Data Analysis Expression (DAX)
  • Project URL: www.powerbi.com

In the realm of the travel and tour industry, understanding customer churn dynamics is paramount for sustained business success. Leveraging Microsoft Power BI, this analysis offers a comprehensive view of customer churn, empowering strategic decision-making for improved customer retention strategies.
This project showcases my proficiency in utilizing Microsoft Power BI to tackle real-world business challenges. Through insightful analysis and interactive visualization, stakeholders gain a deeper understanding of customer churn dynamics and are equipped with actionable insights to drive strategic decision-making in the travel and tour industry.
