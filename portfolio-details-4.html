Healthcare Analytics

  • Overview: This interactive dashboard offers a comprehensive view of healthcare data, focusing on three core areas: Patient Summary, Diagnostic Conditions, and Treatment Cost.
    It helps stakeholders quickly identify trends, track healthcare outcomes, and monitor costs across patient categories.
  • Visualizations: Utilizes diverse chart types including trend lines for temporal patterns, pie charts for patient distribution, bar charts for comparison of treatments, and a heatmap to highlight cost variations.
  • Tool: Tableau & Excel
  • Skills demonstrated: Data storytelling, healthcare analytics, dashboard design, KPI visualization, Tableau best practices.
