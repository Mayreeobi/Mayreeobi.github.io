Healthcare Analytics Dashboard

Project Highlights

This interactive healthcare dashboard provides stakeholders with comprehensive insights into patient care, diagnostic trends, and financial metrics. Built in Tableau, it transforms complex healthcare data into actionable visualizations that support data-driven decision-making for healthcare administrators and policymakers.

The dashboard integrates multiple data sources to present a holistic view of healthcare operations, enabling users to identify patterns in patient outcomes, monitor treatment effectiveness, and optimize cost management across different patient categories and conditions.

Key Features

  • Three integrated views: Patient Summary, Diagnostic Conditions, and Treatment Cost Analysis
  • Trend lines tracking temporal patterns in patient admissions and outcomes
  • Pie charts visualizing patient gender distribution
  • Bar charts comparing treatment costs and effectiveness across conditions
  • Heat maps highlighting seasonal patterns in hospital admissions
  • Interactive filters enabling drill-down analysis by time period, patient type, and diagnosis

Impact

This dashboard empowers healthcare stakeholders to quickly identify trends, track outcomes, and monitor costs, leading to improved resource allocation and better patient care strategies.
