Healthcare Analytics Dashboard

The Challenge

Healthcare administrators and policymakers required a unified, easy-to-interpret view of patient care, diagnostic trends, and treatment costs. Data was fragmented across multiple sources, limiting visibility into patient outcomes, treatment effectiveness, seasonal admission patterns, and cost drivers; slowing down evidence-based decision-making.

What I Built

I designed and delivered an interactive Tableau dashboard that integrates multiple healthcare datasets into a single, analytics-ready view to support operational and strategic decision-making.

  • Prepared and modeled healthcare data in Microsoft Excel for analytics readiness
  • Designed KPI-driven visualizations including trend lines, bar charts, pie charts, and heat maps
  • Built interactive filters enabling drill-down by time period, patient type, and diagnosis
  • Applied Tableau best practices for clarity, usability, and data storytelling

Business Impact:

• Enabled rapid identification of trends in patient admissions and outcomes

• Improved monitoring of treatment effectiveness and cost patterns across conditions

• Revealed seasonal admission trends using heat map analysis

• Reduced decision-making time through real-time access to critical healthcare KPIs

Dashboard Structure

    The solution consists of a three-page Tableau dashboard:

  • Patient Summary – admissions trends, outcomes, and gender distribution;
  • Diagnostic Conditions – prevalence and patterns across medical conditions;
  • Treatment Cost Analysis – comparative cost and effectiveness across diagnoses.
  • Overview Interactive healthcare analytics dashboard providing real-time visibility into patient outcomes, diagnostic trends, and treatment costs. Designed to support data-driven decisions for healthcare administrators and policymakers.
  • Technologies Used Tableau Desktop(Interactive Dashboard), Microsoft Excel, Data Modeling
  • Skills Demonstrated Healthcare analytics, data storytelling, KPI development and visualization, dashboard design, Tableau best practices, interactive reporting, and analytics UX design.
  • Business Impact Enabled healthcare stakeholders to monitor patient care performance, identify cost drivers, and improve resource allocation through centralized, real-time analytics.
