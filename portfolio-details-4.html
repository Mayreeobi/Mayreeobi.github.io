Business Analysis

Project information

  • Tool: Power BI
  • Skills demonstrated: ETL, Data Modelling, Data Analysis Expression (DAX), Quick Measures & Data storytelling
  • Project URL: www.github.com
  • Interactive Dashboard URL: www.powerbi.com

In this project, I utilized Power BI alongside a spectrum of analytical skills to delve into comprehensive business analysis. Leveraging the above skills, I crafted meaningful metrics to evaluate key performance indicators, including total revenue, year-to-date performance, and revenue distribution across different dimensions.
This project served as a platform to track the performance of sales and marketing teams, while simultaneously monitoring revenue trends. By offering insights into sales performance and revenue distribution, stakeholders were equipped to make informed, data-driven decisions.
