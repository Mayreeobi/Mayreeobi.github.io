I’m a Data Analyst with an MBA who thrives at the intersection of business strategy and analytics. My passion lies in uncovering hidden insights and transforming them into strategies that drive measurable growth.

I’ve built dashboards that became essential decision-making tools, automated Excel–Python reports that cut manual work by 50%, and optimized databases for faster, smarter analysis.
